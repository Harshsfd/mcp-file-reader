Artificial Intelligence Notes

1. Introduction
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems. It includes learning, reasoning, and self-correction.

2. Types of AI
- Narrow AI: Designed for a specific task (e.g., Chatbots, Image recognition).
- General AI: A system with generalized human cognitive abilities.
- Super AI: Future AI that surpasses human intelligence.

3. Applications
- Healthcare: Diagnosis and treatment recommendations.
- Finance: Fraud detection, stock trading.
- Education: Personalized learning.
- Autonomous Vehicles: Self-driving cars.

4. Advantages
- Reduces human error
- 24x7 availability
- Faster decision-making

5. Challenges
- High cost of implementation
- Lack of creativity
- Risk of unemployment

Conclusion:
AI is a powerful technology that is transforming industries, but ethical considerations are equally important.
